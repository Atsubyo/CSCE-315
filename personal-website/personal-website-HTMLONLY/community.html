Community

FBLA Color Run

Charity run hosted, planned, and built out by the Cinco Ranch High School FBLA organization for the Katy community. A 5k run in which runners are showered in colors to represent good health and happiness. Efforts of the charity event led to raising thousands of dollars. As a result, the event became an annual staple event at the high school for the entire community of Katy, Texas.

Brookwood Community Service

Biweekly service which involved socializing and aiding in the daily lives of the special needs community of Brookwood. Events held included aerobics, basketball, biking, hiking, and board games. The experience brought new perspectives to the different, but ultimately normal, lives of special needs people ranging ages from young adults to senior members.

Hurricane Harvey Relief

Donations and relief efforts provided to the West Houston area in response to the tragedies of Hurricane Harvey. Support provided included housing support for those who may have lost homes and food donations to those who lacked supply. Offered a new experience and understanding for the effects of natural disasters and the strength of community support that respond in times of need.
